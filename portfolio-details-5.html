Sea Souls Diving Instructor Website details

Project information

  • Category: Web design
  • Client: Sea Souls by Sali
  • Project date: 01 Apr, 2024

I designed and launched a website for Sea Souls Diving Instructor by Sali, a diving center providing captivating underwater experiences. I utilized a combination of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to craft an engaging front-end interface and enhance user experience. Furthermore, I integrated a PHP contact form to facilitate seamless communication with prospective clients and diving enthusiasts seeking services and information.
